About Whitemoon Pro Service LLC

Whitemoon Pro Service LLC is a Central Florida–based event management company specializing in tennis and racquet sports. We partner with academies, clubs, and private facilities to deliver professional, safe, and meticulously organized events for juniors and adults.

Our mission is to elevate the standard of tennis and racquet sports events by providing a complete operational framework—from planning and staffing to communications and reporting—so you can focus on your players and community.

Our Values

  • Professionalism: Every event reflects your brand and ours.
  • Safety: Our entire team is 100% SafePlay™ certified.
  • Integrity: Transparent communication and honest reporting.
  • Excellence: We treat every event as a premium experience.
  • Growth: Focused on Central Florida, expanding nationwide.

Services

We provide end-to-end event management for tennis and racquet sports, tailored to the needs of academies, clubs, and organizations.

Tournament Management (USTA & UTR)

Complete tournament operations from start to finish, including scheduling, draws, on-site management, and player communications.

  • Tournament Director services
  • Certified officials and court monitors
  • Draw creation, scheduling, and scoring
  • Weather delay and match flow management

USTA & UTR Portal Management

We create and manage dedicated event portals on USTA and UTR on behalf of our clients, ensuring a seamless experience for players and parents.

  • Event setup and configuration
  • Player communication through official portals
  • Score entry and match reporting
  • Compliance with platform standards

No experience with USTA/UTR portals? We can run events using our own entity.
