About Whitemoon Pro Service LLC

Whitemoon Pro Service LLC is a Greater Tampa Bay – based event management partner dedicated to tennis and racquet sports. We specialize in running tournaments, match plays, and club events with the precision and care your players, parents, and coaches deserve.

From USTA and UTR portal management to on‑court operations and financial reporting, we provide a complete framework so your organization can focus on development, community, and growth.

What we stand for

  • Professionalism: Every event reflects your brand and ours.
  • Safety: Our team is 100% SafePlay™ certified.
